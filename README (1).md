# 🎬 IMDb Movie Trends Analysis

**Author:** Soumya Shah | [GitHub](https://github.com/SoumyaShahh)

📊 **[👉 Explore the Live Tableau Dashboard](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/soumya.shah6876/viz/MoviesDataAnalysis_17431305476590/DataMoviesIMDbDashboard)**

> An interactive Tableau dashboard built on **526,000+ IMDb movie titles** spanning **1894–2022** — uncovering genre trends, audience engagement patterns, language diversity, and Academy Award insights to support data-driven content strategy decisions.

---

## 📌 Overview

Built as part of the **Tableau x IMDb Data + Movies Challenge**, this dashboard enables entertainment professionals, analysts, and studios to explore global movie trends, identify high-performing genres, and understand audience behavior across decades.

---

## 🔢 Dataset Scale

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Movie Titles | 526,000+ |
| People (cast/crew) | 2.4 million+ |
| Time Span | 1894 – 2022 |
| Source | IMDb (via Tableau) |

## 🔍 Key Dashboard Views

- **Movie Production Over Time** — Growth of global film industry from 1900s to 2022
- **Top 10 Genres** — Genre breakdown by title count and audience preference
- **Language Distribution** — Global reach across 20+ languages
- **Academy Award Winners** — Films with the most Oscar wins
- **Top 2,500 IMDb Movies** — Rating vs. vote count scatter analysis

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- **Drama and Action** dominate both high ratings and award-winning content
- **Post-2000 surge** in production indicates a rapidly expanding global entertainment market
- **English dominates** but rising entries in Hindi, Spanish, and Japanese signal international growth opportunity
- High IMDb ratings correlate strongly with **high vote counts** — audience engagement drives perceived quality
